About Us

Meet Our Founders!

Here at Meal Life we strive to provide a seamless meal prep service that meets all of your needs all in one place. We value quality, fresh ingredients that are sourced locally all over the country and strive to make it an affordable option for families to enjoy.

Founding Members and Executives

Founder and CEO

Darius Williams

Darius is graduate of Ohio State University in Columbus, OH where he posses a Bachelor of Science degree in Nurtition and Exercise Science. He used this background to work as a personal trainer as well as a dietitian to help out clients. He founded Meal Life in 2015 when he saw that even with strong nutrition advice, his clients still struggled to adhere to it because of their busy lives and so he sought to create a service that makes eating clean and healthy simple.

Co-founder and Vice President

Mark Johnson

Mark with his background in business, partnered with Darius to put the idea of Meal Life into action and create a functinal business model around it. He has successfully started and ran three businesses in the past and brings his expertise to the team as acting vice president.

CFO

Brad Smith

Brad is the acting Chief Financial Officer of Meal Life who runs all of the company's financial decisions and oversees financial departments. He has a Bachelor of Science degree in Economics from University of Chicago as well as a MBA.

COO

David Brown

David is Meal Life's Chief Operating Officer and oversees the day to day administrative functions at the company. He has previous experience in the meal prep industry where he brings over a decade of experience with him.
